A Quaint Laboratorium

| lab ~ cafeteria

There lie some abandoned namecards right next to the card reader of a blocked door to the cafeteria. The floor behind seems to have collapsed, but the tags seem shiny and new. All arranged in a slightly chaotic, yet thoughtful way, you might want to make yourself one as well. On a nearby table where some wilted flowers decorate the oddly comforting room, you find empty slates of cards and colorful markers that, hopefully, haven't dried out yet.
